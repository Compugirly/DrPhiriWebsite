My Story

Why I Chose Weight Loss

If you struggle with losing weight and weight regain, you don't need another diet plan or magic supplement. You need proper guidance and a doctor who finds out why your body holds onto weight.

In my practice I help patients lose weight, maintain it, and regain their confidence using realistic, medically-backed methods. The transformation I see when weight loss is treated as the medical issue it truly is — that's why I do this.

Weight gain is not simply about eating too much or moving too little. Hormonal imbalances, insulin resistance, PCOS, thyroid disorders and even genetic factors all play a significant role. These are medical conditions — and they deserve a medical solution.
